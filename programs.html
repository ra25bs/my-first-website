Our Programs & Services

ICFAI offers a variety of programs and services designed to support coffee farmers, improve productivity, and strengthen community development.

Farmer Training

Workshops on sustainable farming, pest management, and coffee quality improvement.

Market Access

Connecting farmers to local and international buyers to maximize profitability.

Sustainable Practices

Promoting organic fertilizers, shade-grown coffee, and eco-friendly farming methods.

Community Projects

Supporting local development initiatives, events, and cooperative programs.

Competitions & Awards

Recognizing outstanding farmers and innovative practices through annual awards.

Educational Support

Providing learning resources, seminars, and guidance for youth and new farmers.
