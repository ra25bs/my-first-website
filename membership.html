Membership Information

How to Become a Member

Fill out the official membership application form and submit it to the ICFAI office along with the required documents. New members are reviewed and approved by the Board of Directors.

Membership Requirements

  • Resident of Malungon, Sarangani Province
  • Active coffee farmer or stakeholder in the industry
  • Completion of membership application form
  • Payment of membership fee (if applicable)

Benefits of Joining

  • Access to training programs and farm tours
  • Participation in community growers program
  • Support for livelihood and marketing initiatives
  • Networking and cooperative benefits

Member Responsibilities

  • Follow ICFAI rules and guidelines
  • Participate in meetings and community programs
  • Contribute to cooperative growth and sustainability
  • Support fellow members and uphold ethical practices
