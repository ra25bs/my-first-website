Organizational Structure

ICFAI operates under a structured leadership system that ensures transparency, collaboration, and effective decision‑making. The organization is composed of the Board of Directors, committees, and farmer-members who work together to oversee operations, projects, and long‑term development.

  • General Assembly – All official members of the association
  • Board of Directors – Oversees policies, programs, and governance
  • Executive Officers – President, Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer
  • Committees – Production, Marketing, Training, Finance, Community Affairs

Mission

To empower the Inag coffee farming community by improving production techniques, ensuring market access, and fostering sustainable livelihoods.

Vision

To be a leading community-driven coffee association recognized for quality, innovation, and social responsibility.

Goals

  • Enhance coffee quality and yield
  • Strengthen farmer education and training
  • Expand market reach and profitability
  • Promote community development and sustainability

Board of Directors & Leaders

The leadership of ICFAI is composed of dedicated community members elected by the General Assembly. Their role is to guide the association’s strategic direction, implement programs, and ensure member welfare.
